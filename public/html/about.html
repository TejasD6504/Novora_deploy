About Us

Our Mission

To design and implement an affordable, scalable, and intelligent warehouse automation system that empowers small and medium enterprises with Industry 4.0 capabilities — improving efficiency, safety, and productivity.

Our Vision

We aim to revolutionize warehouse operations by introducing automation that enhances efficiency, accuracy, and speed. Our goal is to reduce manual dependency, minimize errors, and create a smarter, technology-driven warehouse system accessible to all businesses.

Our Inspiration

Warehouses today face persistent challenges such as delays, inventory mismatches, collision risks, and high labor dependency. Observing how traditional Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) systems remain too costly and complex for small and medium enterprises inspired us to design a solution that is affordable, scalable, and easy to adopt.

Our Approach

Our system integrates low-cost AGVs equipped with IR, Hall Effect, and ultrasonic sensors, combined with centralized path planning on Jetson Nano for efficient and collision-free routing. We enable long-range, low-power LoRaWAN communication for scalability and real-time visual intelligence for dynamic task management.
